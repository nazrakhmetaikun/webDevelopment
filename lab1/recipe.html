Pasta with chicken

  • Time:40 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 300 g
Pasta 200 g
Salt as you want
Sour cream 100 g
Cheese 200 g

Steps Boil some chicken, once it is ready add salt and spices.
Then fry it for 10 mins.
Afterwards, add some water and add spaghetti.
Add sour cream and cheese
Enjoy!

Soup with vegetables

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Beef 500g
Potatoes 150g
Onion 100g
Tomatoes 100 g
Salt and spices as you want

Steps
Boil some meat for 40 mins.
Add salt and spices make sure your meat is well-prepared.
Add all vegetables and boil for 20 mins.
Enjoy!

Fried potatoes

  • Time:15 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Oil 50g
Potatoes 150g
Salt and spices as you want

Steps
Put some oil on your frying pan.
Cut potatoes into thin slices.
Fry your potatoes for 10 mins
Add salt and spices at the end
Enjoy!
